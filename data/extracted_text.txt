Chapter 3: Return, Replacement, and Refund Procedures

Customers can request a return or replacement within 7 days of delivery if the product

- The product is dead on arrival. 
- The product has a major defect. 
- The product is delivered in a damaged condition. 
Please note that for minor issues, repairs will be provided under warranty instead of a

3.2 Refund Policy 
Refunds undergo a strict authorization process to prevent fraud and ensure inventory 
accuracy. Refunds are processed only if: 
- The product is returned in original packaging and condition.

- It is defective. 
- It was damaged during shipping. 
- It was delivered incorrectly. 
Refunds are credited to the original payment method within 7-10 business days after 
inspection. We maintain a strict policy that refunds are not applicable for preference-
based returns (e.g., color choice or performance dissatisfaction).
